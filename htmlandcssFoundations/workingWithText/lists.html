The recipe to make a decent egg and chees sandwich

Egg and cheese sandwich an old recipe that has been passed down the generations from the

1800s to today and that is the recipe we will be making today.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • cheese
  • bread
  • bread

Instructions

  1. Take bread one and place on table
  2. Make an egg
  3. Place made egg on the bread placed earlier
  4. Gather cheese and scatter all over the top of the egg
  5. Take other bread and place on top of cheese
  6. Put cheese and egg sandwich in your mouth and eat
